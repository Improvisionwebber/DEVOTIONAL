PRODUCTS OF GLORY ( PART 11)

OPEN HEAVENS BY GLORY.

26th October - Pastor Kingsley Chigozie Nwekwo

Deuteronomy 28:12 KJV The LORD shall open unto thee his good treasure, the heaven to give the rain unto thy land in his season, and to bless all the work of thine hand: and thou shalt lend unto many nations, and thou shalt not borrow.

Open Heavens speaks of God opening the gates of blessings upon man . In our opening scripture, the Lord himself began to speak about his capacity to open the gates of the blessing upon man in obedience to his words. When the gates of heaven is opened, we receive access to the wealth, the blessing, the dominion and the favour of the Lord.

When Glory shows up, men and systems are subjected and dominated by its operations and manifestation.

WHAT ARE THE BENEFITS OF OPEN HEAVENS BY GLORY.

1) DIVINE VISITATION:- Everytime the Heavens opens, Divinity comes to fellowship with humanity. When God opens the heavens he grants access to his presence either by his voice, angelic visitation, by his spirit or by his presence. Everytime the Heavens open, God comes with a divine agenda, an assignment for a man, a purpose to be fulfilled here in earth.

2)OPEN HEAVENS BRINGS SUPERNATURAL SUPPLIES.The Children of Israel in the wilderness enjoyed supernatural supplies from open Heaven. The Lord released Manna to them from the kitchen of divinity in Ex 16 : 14, Psalm 78 :4 . When Elijah's supply from the brook Cherith ran dry, God sent him to the Widow of Zarephath whose cruse of oil and supply of flour never ran dry because there was Heavenly supply. 1kings 17: 18, Open heavens brings supply from glory. Phil. 4 : 19

3)OPEN HEAVENS BRINGS SUPERNATURAL MANIFESTATION.In the book of 1kings 18 : 38, the heavens were opened to release fire upon the sacrifice of Elijah. A supernatural manifestation that affirmed the credibility of the one true God was manifested through open heaven.When Glory opens the heavens , supernatural manifestations beyond human comprehension are seen. Men are left in awe of a power that is beyond their control.The Red Sea was parted by Glory in the book of Exodus, in order to make a way of escape for the Children of Israel from their pursuers. When heaven opens Glory does the unimaginable.

4) Open Heavens releases the Rain of the blessing. Isaiah 55:10. When heaven is opened, God releases his abundant rain to water the earth.in 1 kings18: 45, God caused the heavens to open over Israel to release the rain of healing and cancel the famine in the land.

5) Open Heaven releases divine Judgement.God judged Egypt with hail stones from heaven and brought great destruction to Egypt in Ex.9: 24.. In Gen 7: 22- 12.,God opened the windows of heaven and judged the wickedness of man for forty days and forty nights with rain.The captains of Fifty and their host were judged with fire from heaven that roasted them 2 kings 1:11-12.

2 Peter 1:17 For he received from God the Father honour and glory, when there came such a voice to him from the excellent glory, This is my beloved Son, in whom I am well pleased.

6) GLORY REVERSES IMPOSSIBLE SITUATION OF POVERTY - 2 KGS 7:1Glory carries the capacity to abolish poverty and bring forth prosperity. In Luke 5 : 6 , when glory showed up in the person of Jesus, scarcity in the boat of Peter was destroyed. Glory destroys scarcity and brings abundance. 2 cor 8 :9.

{% endcomment %}

Note: Glory commands Open Heavens for divine release.
