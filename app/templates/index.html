Reversing Delay and Activating Divine Speed (Part 11)

THE MANTLE OF YOUR PROPHET

10th October - Pastor Kingsley Chigozie Nwekwo

Exodus 2:16-18 NIV: Now a priest of Midian had seven daughters, and they came to draw water and fill the troughs to water their father’s flock.[17] Some shepherds came along and drove them away, but Moses got up and came to their rescue and watered their flock.[18] When the girls returned to Reuel their father, he asked them, “Why have you returned so early today?”

From our Anchor Scripture, the Daughter of Reuel suffered delay in fetching of water every other day because they lacked a Shepherd to fight for them against evil shepherd , before Moses appeared.The assignment of Moses for Reuel's Daughters was more of an intercession and a Pastor.The mantle of the Prophet is for fighting the battles of his people committed under him by God .

{% comment %}

I Prophesy into your destiny, let every evil protocol of delay against your life be reversed now in Jesus Name .Let the spirit of favour grant you speed in Jesus Name.

{% endcomment %} {% comment %}

Also very instructive, in latter verses of Mark five, is the capacity of the living Christ as our fountain of restoration to heal and reverse hard issues of afflictions.

{% endcomment %} {% comment %}

The power of God reversed the issue of blood assigned to waste the destiny of the woman . It can also be said that the power of God restored speed to that woman. Further still, and even more inspiring, her faith in God was key to activating the power of God for her restoration.

{% endcomment %}

How do the Ministry of your Pastor terminates delay and activates Speed?

1) Your Pastor carries Prophetic Power to activate speed. Hosea 12:13 ;And by a prophet the LORD brought Israel out of Egypt, and by a prophet was he preserved.

2) Your God ordained Prophet carries the truth, the light and the spirit of God that destroys the spirit of delay over your life.Jeremiah 3:15 ;And I will give you pastors according to mine heart, which shall feed you with knowledge and understanding.When Israelites were delivered from Egypt, they met the water Marah which was undrinkable. This was a manifestation of the spirit of delay against the People of God but they were under the Prophetic Wisdom of Moses. He sought God and God shown him a tree ( a light) that arrested that situation and the water was made sweet . The power of delay was broken by the Prophetic covering of Moses. Exodus 15:25 ;

3) The Prophet releases the anointing that destroys the yoke of delay .- 2 Kings 9:1-5 Peter struggled all night and caught nothing untill the anointing of Jesus manifested and cancel that delay by His Word.- Luke 5:1-5.

4) Your God ordained Prophet seeks your gain and speed in life- Isaiah 56;10-12..The Barrenness of Rebekah was destroyed by the Prophetic prayers of the husband Isaac. Abraham was delayed many years, when the turn of Isaac came, after 20 years of delay, he stood in gap and pray and that spirit of delay was casted out.

5) The Prophetic mantle over your life destroys limiting wall of Jericho.- Josh 6:20

6) The Prophet over your life restore your missing axehead of speed by his mantle of power and wisdom.-2 Kings 6:1-6.

7) The mantle of the Prophet over your life restores your delayed enthronement and crowns. 2 Kings 9:1-5

Note: Your God ordained Prophet is God 's major weapon against the Spirit of delay against your life.

LORD , THANK YOU FOR THE GIFT OF MY PASTOR, I RECEIVE THE ALIGNMENT OF SUBMISSION TO OBEY HIS INSTRUCTION IN JESUS NAME.
