PRODUCTS OF GLORY (PT 12)

ARISE AND SHINE BY GLORY

27th October - Pastor Kingsley Chigozie Nwekwo

Isaiah 60:1 AMP â€œArise [from spiritual depression to a new life], shine [be radiant with the glory and brilliance of the LORD]; for your light has come, And the glory and brilliance of the LORD has risen upon you.. The glory of the Lord brings an "arising" from the dust of hopelessness and wretchedness to the shining forth as "bright lights" of glory

It is the greatness of God's Glory that settles the poor with thrones of greatness. The Hand of God moves the poor from smallness to greatness using glory of God as its platform.1 Samuel 2:8; He raiseth up the poor out of the dust, and lifteth up the beggar from the dunghill, to set them among princes, and to make them inherit the throne of glory:

I speak as an Oracle of the Most High God;No matter the strength of darkness holding your destiny, in the Name of Jesus, I command your life to shine forth in glory. These evil barriers and yokes are destroyed by fire in Jesus Name.

When Glory shows up, men and systems are subjected and dominated by its operations and manifestation.

{% endcomment %} {% comment %}

The power of God reversed the issue of blood assigned to waste the destiny of the woman . It can also be said that the power of God restored speed to that woman. Further still, and even more inspiring, her faith in God was key to activating the power of God for her restoration.

Let's consider just two Biblical Characters that Glory caused to "Arise and Shine" in Destiny.

1) ABRAHAMActs 7:2 ; The God of glory appeared unto our father Abraham, when he was in Mesopotamia, before he dwelt in Charran,Abraham was 75 years in his father's house as a failure with wife that is barren, when the glory mantle of "Arise and shine" encountered him as seen in Acts. 7:2He arose from that state of barrenness, poverty, backwardness, untimely death, failure and evil pattern of his father's house ( Terah) to shining forth in glory by the Power of the blessing. - Genesis 12:2 And I will make of thee a great nation, and I will bless thee, and make thy name great; and thou shalt be a blessing:Abraham shined so much by the force of the blessing that God make him a great Name , he became a divine signature and heavenly brand of the blessing.-Further still, Jesus's assignment of redemption in addition to salvation, was deliverance from curse of the Law and restoring the blessing of Abraham upon us.- Deut 28:1-13., Galatians 3:13

2) JOSEPHJoseph was from the beginning a vessel of favour and increase as his name implies but that attracted early battle due to envy of his brothers. Furthermore, the father's Love was more for Joseph as such special coat of many colours was given to him by Jacob his father.And this increase their envy and anger against Joseph.More still, Joseph had a dream of greatness and dominion over his brethren that was revealed to them out of childishness. The brothers hated him the more for his dream- Gen. 37:3Furthermore, they conspired against him and threw Joseph into a season of betrayal and slavery. That was a season of dunghill for Joseph. He served as slave to Potiphar, then to the Prison for Rape he did not commit.At this Point Glory of "Arise and shine" came for Joseph , the King sent for him because "Arising and shining Glory" has come for him to be lifted and enthroned.Genesis 41:14;Then Pharaoh sent and called Joseph, and they brought him hastily out of the dungeon: and he shaved himself, and changed his raiment, and came in unto Pharaoh.When Joseph encountered the shining Glory, all answers and favour needed for his enthronement by Pharaoh , was packaged into Joseph by the glory of God .- Acts 7:10.Joseph shined and became the Prime Minister who commanded so much honour and glory in Egypt.Genesis 41:41-42 KJVAnd Pharaoh said unto Joseph, See, I have set thee over all the land of Egypt. [42] And Pharaoh took off his ring from his hand, and put it upon Joseph's hand, and arrayed him in vestures of fine linen, and put a gold chain about his neck;

3)OPEN HEAVENS BRINGS SUPERNATURAL MANIFESTATION.In the book of 1kings 18 : 38, the heavens were opened to release fire upon the sacrifice of Elijah. A supernatural manifestation that affirmed the credibility of the one true God was manifested through open heaven.When Glory opens the heavens , supernatural manifestations beyond human comprehension are seen. Men are left in awe of a power that is beyond their control.The Red Sea was parted by Glory in the book of Exodus, in order to make a way of escape for the Children of Israel from their pursuers. When heaven opens Glory does the unimaginable.

In Gen 7: 22- 12.,God opened the windows of heaven and judged the wickedness of man for forty days and forty nights with rain.The captains of Fifty and their host were judged with fire from heaven that roasted them 2 kings 1:11-12.

2 Peter 1:17 For he received from God the Father honour and glory, when there came such a voice to him from the excellent glory, This is my beloved Son, in whom I am well pleased.
